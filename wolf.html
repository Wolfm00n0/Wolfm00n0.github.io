Wolves

Wolves otherwise known as Canis Lupus are found in mostly Canada, The United States particularly Alaska, Greenland, Russia, and Northen Europe. A few species of wolf can be also found in South America

Wolf Breeds

There are 26 different breeds of wolves.

  1. Alaskan Interior Wolf (Canis lupus pambasileus)
  2. Alaskan Tundra Wolf (Canis lupus tundrarum)
  3. Alexander Archipelago Wolf (Canis lupus ligoni)
  4. Arabian Wolf (Canis lupus arabs)
  5. Arctic Wolf (Canis lupus arctos)
  6. Baffin Island Wolf (Canis lupus manningi)
  7. British Columbian Wolf (Canis lupus columbianus)
  8. Eastern Wolf (Canis lupus lycaon)
  9. Eurasian Wolf (Canis lupus lupus)
  10. Greenland Wolf (Canis lupus orion)
  11. Himalayan Wolf (Canis lupus chanco)
  12. Hudson Bay Wolf (Canis lupus hudsonicus)
  13. Iberian Wolf (Canis lupus signatus)
  14. Indian Wolf (Canis lupus pallipes)
  15. Italian Wolf (Canis lupus italicus)
  16. Labrador Wolf (Canis lupus labradorius)
  17. Mackenzie River Wolf (Canis lupus mackenzii)
  18. Mexican Wolf (Canis lupus baileyi)
  19. Mongolian Wolf (Canis lupus chanco)
  20. New Guinea Singing Dog (Canis lupus dingo)
  21. Northern Rocky Mountain Wolf (Canis lupus irremotus)
  22. Northwestern Wolf (Canis lupus occidentalis)
  23. Red Wolf (Canis lupus rufus)
  24. Steppe Wolf (Canis lupus campestris)
  25. Tundra Wolf (Canis lupus albus)
  26. Vancouver Island Wolf (Canis lupus crassodon)
